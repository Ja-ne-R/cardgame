Game info and how to play:

Click on draw cards to get 1 random card. Your hand can hold 4 cards

There are 2 types of cards, monster cards and support item cards

Monster cards have health(HP) and attack(ATK), and points which the defeater of the monster gets.

Support item cards(SP) have special effects.
